<p>Følger du #koha på Twitter? Da bør du lese dette, som Nicole Engard sendte ut på Koha-postlistene for ikke lenge siden:</p>
<blockquote><p>Most of us have been using the #koha hash tag on Twitter for our Koha related tweets, but recently a newspaper with that name has started sending out massive numbers of tweets hiding our Koha related stories. It was discussed on IRC earlier this week so I thought I’d share with those who weren’t online at the time that we’re now going to use #kohails for our Koha related tweets.</p></blockquote>
